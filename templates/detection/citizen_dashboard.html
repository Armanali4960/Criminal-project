How It Works

  1. Upload Photo: Take a photo of a person or upload an existing image
  2. Use Camera: Click "Use Camera" to capture a live photo with your device camera
  3. Detection: Our system scans the image for faces
  1. Matching: Faces are compared against our criminal database
  2. Alert: If a match is found, police are immediately notified
Tip: For best results, use clear, well-lit photos with faces clearly visible. The camera feature works best in good lighting conditions.

Privacy Notice

All images are processed securely and deleted after analysis unless a criminal is detected.
